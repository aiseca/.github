# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

AISECA is committed to providing a welcoming and professional environment for all contributors, regardless of background or experience level.

## Standards

**Expected behaviour:**
- Professional and constructive communication
- Respect for differing viewpoints and experiences
- Vendor-neutral, evidence-based contributions
- Graceful acceptance of constructive feedback

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Promotion of specific commercial products disguised as standards contributions
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to conduct@aiseca.org. All reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.
